GETTING TESTED

  • In our COVID19-test facility we offer only one type of test, the PCR-test.
  • You must book an appointment in due time, in order to get tested.
  • It is not allowed to simply walk in without an appointment.
  • Remember to wear a facemask or other government approved health safety standard.
  • There might be a queue, in this case please wait 5-10 min before entering.

PCR TEST

  • The PCR test is very sensitive and will turn a positive result after 3-10 days of infection.
  • The PCR test can also be positive for some days till weeks after you are healthy again.
  • The test therefore cannot be used to determine when you are healthy.
  • If you did not have any symptoms, you are then non-contagieous 7 days after the test has come back positive.
